## 📊 Payroll Risk Analytics Dashboard

### 🔍 Overview
This project analyzes payroll data to identify financial risk exposure and highlight where high-risk issues are concentrated across departments.

---

### 🎯 Key Insights
- Low-risk transactions drive total exposure but are operationally manageable  
- High-risk exposure is concentrated in HR, Compliance, and IT  
- Targeted controls in these departments can reduce financial risk most effectively  

---

### 📈 Dashboard Features
- Total Payroll Exposure: $354K  
- High-Risk Exposure: $24.6K  
- Critical Risk Cases: 4  
- Risk distribution by level  
- High-risk exposure by department
